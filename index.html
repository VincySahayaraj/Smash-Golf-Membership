Simulators

Smash Factor Lounge offers three types of
simulator experience:

Driving Range

Designed for golfer’s improvement, our range features Golfzon’s Driving Range Simulator, or GDR for short. The GDR simulator features a high-resolution dual camera sensor system which recognizes ball launch data such as speed, angle direction, and back/side spin rate.

Premium Bay

Is an individually located, somewhat private simulator which features Golfzon’s Vision Premium Simulator. The Vision Premium offer’s Golfzon’s vision software which virtually transports you to over 190 golf courses around the world.

Luxury Suite

This fully private, luxury suite features the best simulator money can buy. Golfzon’s Two Vision simulator offers everything the Vision Premium has, and more!
